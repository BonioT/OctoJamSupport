Frequently Asked Questions

How does the sync work?

OctoJam uses custom network algorithms to calculate and adjust for latency between devices, ensuring audio is played at the exact same moment on all phones.

Do I need Wi-Fi or Internet?

No internet connection is required. OctoJam uses a local mesh network via Wi-Fi and Bluetooth to connect devices directly.

How many phones can I sync?

You can sync multiple devices! The more phones that join, the louder and more immersive the sound becomes.

Is my data private?

Absolutely. OctoJam does not collect any personal data or track what you listen to. Everything happens locally on your devices.
