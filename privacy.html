Privacy Policy for OctoJam

Last updated: April 17, 2026

At OctoJam, we believe that your privacy is paramount. This policy explains how our application handles information.

1. Data Collection

OctoJam does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All synchronization and audio data remain entirely on your local device or are transmitted only between devices connected via your local network (Wi-Fi or Bluetooth).

2. Local Connectivity

The app uses Apple's Multipeer Connectivity framework to discover and synchronize with nearby devices. This process happens locally and does not involve the internet or any third-party tracking services.

3. Audio Data

OctoJam plays audio from the URLs you provide. We do not track what you listen to, nor do we store your listening history.

4. Children's Privacy

Since we do not collect any data, OctoJam is safe for users of all ages and complies with COPPA and GPDR standards regarding data privacy.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
